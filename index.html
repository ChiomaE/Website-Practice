Ateez

A Beginner's Guide

Ateez is a South Korean boy band formed by KQ Entertainment. The group consists of eight members: Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung and Jongho. They debuted on October 24, 2018, with the EP Treasure EP.1: All to Zero.

Quick Facts

  • The group consists of eight members: Hongjoong, Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung and Jongho.
  • ATEEZ is an acronym for "A TEEnager Z", It also stands for "A to Z" meaning everyone, they are a group of teenagers who are aiming to do everything they can enthusiastically.
  • The fandom name for ATEEZ is "ATINY." This comes from a combination of "ATEEZ" and "destiny," meaning that the fans and the group are destined to meet.
